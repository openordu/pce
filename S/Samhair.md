``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Offspring of the legendary hero Fionn Mac Cumhaill, Samhair was an Irish heroine who married Cormac Cas. In an effort to please his wife, Cormac Cas built a bed held by three massive pillars, which inspired the name of their palace - Dún-tri-lag (present-day Duntryleague in Co. Limerick).

:::
```
