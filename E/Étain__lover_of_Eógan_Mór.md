``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Étain, the lover of Eógan Mór, was known as a Fairy woman. She saved her beloved, the king of Munster, when he was defeated by Conn of the Hundred Battles. Étain and her lover's men found shelter in her home situated in Inis Grecraige or Beare Island in Bantry Bay, where she cared for them until they regained their strength to get back into the battle.

:::
```
