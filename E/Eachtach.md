``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
The brave Eachtach was an Irish heroine who fought against Fionn Mac Cumhaill, lord of her father Diarmait Ua Duibne, and his beloved wife Gráinne. This courageous act was in response to Fionn's refusal to aid Diarmait in his fight against a mystical Boar. Furthermore, it is worth noting that Eachtach is connected to the mythical creature known as the Water Horse, also called each uisce or each uisge.

:::
```
