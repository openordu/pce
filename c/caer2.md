caer
caer
n.f. wall, castle, citadel. Common element of Welsh placenames; analogous to "chester." In The Chronicles of Narnia, the placename of Cair Paravel is derived from the Welsh word caer. [Welsh; compare with the Irish cathair and Latin castra]
