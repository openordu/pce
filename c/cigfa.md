Cigfa
Cigfa
The wife of Pryderi, who is left behind with her father-in-law Manawyddan when Pryderi and Rhiannon are abducted.
She doesn't play a large role, and only serves as a way to give an example of Manawyddan's virtues of chastity and perseverence.
