``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Welsh hero, March fab Merchiawn, served as the Welsh equivalent to the legendary king Mark of Tristan and Iseult. March was among the “Three Seafarers of the Island of Britain,” arriving by boat from the Norseman land to offer counsel to his cousin, King Arthur, from time to time.

:::
```
