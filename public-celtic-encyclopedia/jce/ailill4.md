Ailill Agach (Edge of Battle)
Father of the hero Máel Dúin. While on a rampage, he raped a nun, who became pregnant with Máel Dúin. Ailill is then killed by some Leinstermen, and Máel Dúin must struggle with himself whether to avenge his father's death.
