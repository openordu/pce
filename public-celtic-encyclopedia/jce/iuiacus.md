Iuiacus
Iuiacus/Mercury Iuiacus
Gaulish deity
His name means either "Yew-god" or "God of the festival," if iuos from the Coligny Calendar does mean "festival," as some believe.  If "Yew-god," he would be similar to the gods Abello, god of apple trees, and Fagus, god of beech trees.
