Mercury Senus
Senus
AKA Mercury Senus
"M. the Old"
Why a god like Mercury would be associated with being elderly, I don't know, except perhaps it reflects the idea that Ogmios, god of eloquence, when not identified with Hercules, is often depicted as an old man.
