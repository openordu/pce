The Shivering Boy
The Shivering Boy
A ghost said to haunt Triermain Castle, in Northumberland.  He is a small boy with icy fingers, heard to say, "Cold, cold, forever more."  He is thought to be a fifteenth century inheritor of the castle who was killed by his greedy uncle, a story which echoes the Two Princes in the Tower of London.
