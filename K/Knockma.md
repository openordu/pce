``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Hill near Tuam, Co. Galway, known as Knockma (Cnoc Meadha, Cnoc Mheadha) and named after Medb, is renowned as the mound of Finnbheara, the Fairy king of Connacht. Finnbheara resided there with his fairy spouse, Úna, also known as Nuala. Presently, this Irish mythological site goes by the name of Castle Hackett. See <TOPIC> for more information.

:::
```
