::: magazinestyle
Depicted in Celtic art are various goddesses such as Epona, the horse goddess, and the Deae Matres, the mother goddesses, who are often portrayed holding keys. These keys are believed to be a significant symbol, indicating a joyous transition from the earthly existence to the realm of the afterlife.

:::
