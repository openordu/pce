``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
A British or Roman goddess named Ollototae is mentioned in an inscription discovered in Chester. It is unknown whether this deity was originally a Celtic goddess who was later transformed into a Roman goddess, or if the Roman goddess was an adaptation of the Celtic goddess of the earth.

:::
```
