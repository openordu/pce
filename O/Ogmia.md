``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
A curly-haired youth with rays erupting from his head, Ogmia was a British god, thought to be the British counterpart of Ogmios, the Celtic god of eloquence on the continent, and Ogma, the Irish god. Although depicted by Romano-Celtic artists, there is currently no information available regarding the myth or cult of Ogmia.

:::
```
