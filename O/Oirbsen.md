``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
An obscure term used for the shape-shifting god of the sea, Mannanán Mac Lir, who drowned in a lake which is currently known as Lough Corrib is Oirbsen, an alternate name or title for this Irish god.

:::
```
