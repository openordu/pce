``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Three women of King Arthur's court were celebrated in Welsh Triads as the Three Splendid Women, all of whom were Welsh heroines. The model wife Enid, faithful and challenging, along with the helpful Tegau Eurfron, and the beautiful Dyfr, although not widely known, were admired for their virtues.

:::
```
