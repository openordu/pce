Abello
Abello
Alternately: Abellio, Abelio
"apple"
Gaulish god of apple trees; dedications to him are mostly found in Haut-Garonne, with one in Aquitaine.
His name is related to the Welsh Afallach, father of Modron and ancestor of several Welsh royal houses according to the genealogies in Harleian 3859.
