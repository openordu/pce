Ayglin
Ayglin
In Le Roman d'Escanor, the uncle of Andrivete, who usurps the throne of Northumbria after the death of Andrivete's father King Canor.  He attempts to force Andrivete to marry and keep away from the throne, but she runs off with Kay, who helps her regain the throne.
