A Medieval Arthurian Reading List and Sourcebook
A Medieval Arthurian Reading List and Sourcebook
In my efforts to learn as much as possible about the Arthurian Mythos, I have collected quite a few books on the subject.  I feel it would only be good to tell others what medieval texts are currently in print, or are available online.
Welsh
The Four Ancient Books of Wales
Black Book of Carmarthen
Book of Aneurin
Book of Taliesin
Red Book of Hergest
The Mabinogion: print. various, but look for the Guest version, which includes the Hanes Taliesin.
Trioedd Ynys Prydein: ed. Rachel Bromwich.
Latin
Historia Brittonum: Nennius: net--http://www.celtic-twilight.com or http://www.yorku.ca/inpar/Arthurian.html
The History of the Kings of Britain: Geoffrey of Monmouth: print/net (various)
Vita Merlini: Geoffrey of Monmouth.  net: http://www.geocities.com/branwaedd/dragon/bards/merlin/merlini.html
Art of Courtly Love: Andreas Capellanus: print, Columbia.
French
The Romances of Chretien de Troyes: trans. David Stains. print, IUP.
Erec et Enid
Cliges
Yvain
Lancelot
Perceval
Merlin and the Grail (Le Roman du Graal): Robert de Boron: print, Bowden. net: Didot Perceval portion on E2.
Lancelot of the Lake--the Old French Lancelot: trans. Corin Corley: print, Oxford.
The Lais of Marie de France: Marie de France: print, Penguin.
Three Arthurian Romances: trans. Ross. G. Arthur: print, Everyman.
Caradoc
The Perilous Graveyard
The Knight with the Sword
Jaufre: trans. Ross G. Arthur: print, Garland.
Tyolet: trans. Ross G. Arthur: net, http://www.yorku.ca/inpar/Arthurian.html. Or, trans. Jessie Weston, net: http://www.geocities.com/branwaedd/tyolet.html
Sir Gawain and the Lady of Lys: trans. Jessie Weston. net: http://www.geocities.com/branwaedd/lys.html
Sir Gawain at the Grail Castle: trans. Jessie Weston. net: http://www.geocities.com/branwaedd/gawain.html
both from The Second Perceval Continuation
from Diu Krone.
The Lancelot - Grail Cycle
The Quest of the Holy Grail
The Death of Arthur
The Elucidation prologue to Perceval: trans. Stephen Evans.
Tristan: Beroul. print, various.
Guingamour, Lanval, Tyolet, Le Bisclaveret: trans. Jessie L. Weston. 
German
Parzival: Wolfram von Eschenbach. print, various.
Titurel: Wolfram von Eschenbach. printed with new edition of Parzival
Tristan and Iseult: Gottfried von Strassburg. print, various.
Diu Krone:  soon to be reprinted.
Wigalois: Wirnt Von Gravenberg. in print.
Middle English
Sir Gawain and the Green Knight: print, various.
The Athurian Chronicles of Wace and La3amon: print, Everyman.
The Alliterative Mort Arthur: print, Penguin.
Le Mort d'Arthur: Sir Thomas Malory: print, various.
Sir Gawain: Eleven Romances and Tales: ed. Thomas Hahn. print, Western Michigan. net: http://www.lib.rochester.edu/camelot/gawmenu.htm
Dutch
Lanzelet: Ulrich von Zatzikhoven. poss. in print
Sir Morien: trans. Jessie Weston. WWW.
Miscellaneous Collections:
The Romance of Arthur: ed. James J. Wilhelm.  contains some of above, as well as:
The Saga of the Mantle
Beroul's Tristan
The Suite du Merlin (a continuation of de Boron's Merlin)
The Rise of Gawain
The Wedding of Sir Gawain and Dame Ragnell
The TEAMS Camelot Project: an online collection of Middle English Arthurian texts.
