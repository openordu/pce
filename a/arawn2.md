Arawn ap Cynfarch
Arawn ap Cynfarch
487 - ?
King North of the Salway, 
The brother of Urien of Rheged.
In the History of the Kings of Britain and Arthurian legend, he becomes King Auguselus of Scotland, while his brother becomes Uriens of Gorre.
