Ailill Áine
Ailill Áine
Son of high king Loegaire Lorc, he was slain by his jealous uncle Cobthach the Meager, who wanted the throne of Ireland. However, Cobthach missed Ailill's son Moen Ollam, who avenged his death and regained the throne.
