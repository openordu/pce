St. Ia
Saint Ia
Feast: February 3
Irish saint
Legend has this princess floating to Cornwall on a cabbage leaf.  She was coming to Cornwall with her companions in the year 460 A.D., but became seperated.  She came to shore at what is now the Cornish town of St. Ives.
