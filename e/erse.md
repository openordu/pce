Erse
Erse
An English word for the Irish language, popular in the nineteenth century, though not generally used by the Irish themselves, who saw it as a slur.  It originally refered to Scottish Gaelic, but later came to refer to Irish itself.
I can't help but notice that it sounds similar to the word arse.
