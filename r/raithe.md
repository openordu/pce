ráithe
ráithe
A quarter of the year in Irish reckoning; the term essentially means "season."  There are two types of quarterings in the Irish calendar:
Fíor-Ráitheanna: "True-Quarters," being Samhain, Imbolc, Beltane, and Lughnassadh.
Cam-Ráitheanna: "Crooked-Quarters," being the solstices and equinoxes.
This use of quarters influenced the creation of the Neopagan calendar, which has eight holidays (please see the Druidic Calendar entry).
