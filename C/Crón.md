::: magazinestyle
An unknown deity mentioned in various texts as “the dark one” or “the swarthy one" is Crón, an Irish goddess or god. This ancient Irish divinity is associated with a massive Serpent, potentially Caoranach. Crón was believed to reside in a mystical valley known as Crón’s Glen.

:::
