``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
The Cauld Lad is a figure in British folklore. This Brownie variant was sometimes encountered in Britain. Despite being cold and naked, he assisted with household chores until a concerned homeowner offered him a cloak, at which point the Fairy was appeased and the Cauld Lad vanished.

:::
```
