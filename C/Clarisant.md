::: magazinestyle
Arthurian heroine Clarisant, also known as Clarisse, was the daughter of Morgause, King Arthur's half-sister, and the king of Orkney Island, a remote northern island. Clarisant resided in an enchanting castle with her lover, Guireomelant. Additionally, Gawain, one of the knights of the Round Table, was Clarisant's brother.

:::
