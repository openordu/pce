``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Located atop a mountain in Merionethshire, the Caer Idris is a significant site from Welsh mythology. Its rocks possess an aura of mysticism, with tales revolving around the formation's supposed magical prowess. Legend states that those who spend a night seated on the stone chair shall greet the morning as either a madman or a lyrical genius.

:::
```
