``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Arthurian hero, known for his cruelty, encountered the Jewish merchant Joseph of Arimathea upon his arrival in Britain with the Grail, which was the cup used by Jesus Christ during his last supper. Crudel, the pagan king, imprisoned Joseph, only to be later released by Christian warriors.

:::
```
