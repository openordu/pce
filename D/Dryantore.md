``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Dryantore, an Irish hero, sought revenge against Fionn Mac Cumhaill and his warrior group, the Fianna, after they killed his three sons and Ailna's husband. Ailna created a Druid's Fog which ensnared the murderers, allowing Dryantore to capture Fionn and his harper, Dáire. He imprisoned the two in his Otherworld palace. However, the rest of the warrior band found and freed them, leading to a battle in which Dryantore was defeated and killed.

:::
```
