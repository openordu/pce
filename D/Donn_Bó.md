``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Irish hero Donn Bó was well-known for his melodious singing. He was a brave warrior and met his death when he was killed and beheaded, as per the custom. However, his Head was presented in a victory banquet and placed on a pillar. Unexpectedly, the Head started to sing such a soulful and exquisite melody that it instantly moved everyone present to tears.

:::
```
