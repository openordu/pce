::: magazinestyle
attributed as Dubhlinn, which means "black pool" in Irish. Dub is revered as a powerful figure and is thought to have been a symbol of sovereignty for the Celts. The city of Dublin has a rich history tied to this legendary figure, and her influence can still be felt in the city's cultural traditions today.

:::
