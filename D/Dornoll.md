::: magazinestyle
Dornoll (Dornolla) was a Scottish heroine who was the daughter of Domnall Míldemail, the king of Scotland. Although she was powerful, she was not attractive. Dornoll was a Druid and a warrior woman. She fell in love with the Irish hero, Cúchulainn, however, he rejected her and chose to study with another Scottish Amazon named Scáthach.

:::
