``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Continental Celtic deity known as Nemetius. Scholars have inferred his divinity from the grammatical structure of his Latinized name. The west bank of the Rhine was the location of one site where Nemetius was worshipped. Nemetius’ name is associated with Celtic terms for “sacred grove,” which can also be found in Nemeton and NemetonA.

:::
```
