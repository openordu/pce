::: magazinestyle
A Scottish folkloric figure that goes by the name of Nicnevin is sometimes referred to as Habetrot in other sources. Nicnevin or Habetrot is depicted as a beautiful Fairy Queen who disguises herself as the leader of the Wild Hunt. This hunt is composed of fairies who steal people from their homes to become entertainments for the fairy community.

:::
