``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Arthurian hero Lamorack de Galles was known for his prowess in battle and his membership in the Round Table, being the son of King Pellinore. In an act of aggression, he slayed the husband of King Arthur's half-sister Morgause. In retribution, Morgause's sons, including Gawain, sought revenge and ultimately killed Lamorack de Galles.

:::
```
