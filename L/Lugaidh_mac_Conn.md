``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Fostered by Conn and an adversary of Ailill Olom, the ruler of Munster, was Lugaidh mac Conn. In a critical battle waged in Mag Mucramhan, Lugaidh vanquished Ailill's army. Subsequently, Lugaidh attempted to reconcile with the Munster monarch, however, Ailill's poisoned breath proved fatal and Lugaidh met his demise.

:::
```
