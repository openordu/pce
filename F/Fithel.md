``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Irish hero Fithel (also known as Fítheal, Fíothal, and Fithil) had a crucial role in Irish tradition as a knowledgeable judge or Brehon. Fithel was responsible for resolving the conflicts of the Fianna, a group of warriors who served his brother Fionn Mac Cumhaill. Later on, Fithel left to serve King Cormac Mac Airt at Tara.

:::
```
