``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
The poet of the Fianna, Fergus Fínbél, acted as an advisor to Fionn Mac Cumhaill. While not as revered as the beloved Oisín, some sources suggest that Fergus is, in fact, Fionn's father.

:::
```
