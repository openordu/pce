``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Contemporary religion known as Faerie (also spelled Fairy or Feri) is categorized under the Celtic Revival although it is only remotely associated with Celtic beliefs. Instead, it is based on the folkloric representations of the weakened deities of the Fairy kingdom. (See Neo-Pagan.)

:::
```
