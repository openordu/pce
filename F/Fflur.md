``` tab [group1:Info]
::: magazinestyle
Obscure and enigmatic, Fflur is a Welsh goddess or heroine. The Welsh Triads recount that she was the beloved of Caswallawn, a hero who battled Julius Caesar for her affections. The connection between Fflur and Blodeuwedd, the flowermaiden, is possible given their similar names. Additionally, the existence of two competing monarchs in the legend points towards a potential link to the goddess of Sovereignty. However, Fflur's legend has been all but forgotten, and its significance remains elusive.

:::
```
