Bodiocasses
Bodiocasses
Also, Baiocasses or Uadikasioi
Tribe around Bayeux, who likely gave their name to the area.  The name is possibly cognate of Middle Irish buidechas "blond-haired", which would fit with the classical description of the Celts.
