Brigantes
Brigantes
loc.: Yorkshire, UK
A tribe centered in what is now Yorkshire, England; it is probably related to the Brigantes of Ireland, and may be related to the Brigantii of Rhaetia, in what is now Austria, and the Brigantii of Celtiberia.
